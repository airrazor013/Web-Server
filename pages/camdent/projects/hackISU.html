Hack ISU 2018 - SECRET


Steganography/Encryption for Circumventing REalized Tyranny - Encrypt and embed messages accross multiple devices.

Our project won the 1st place prize for hardware, as well as the 1st place prize for most ethical hack.

During this event, I was tasked with setting up the hardware required for the project, as well as coding the microcontroler.
The microcontroler took atmospheric noise as an input. There were 8 data lines connected from the microcontroler to the Raspbery Pi.
These lines could be polled at any time to construct a byte that would be used as part of the encryption.
Once the device had been polled enough times, it would create a keys from the bytes received. These keys were then used to preform RSA encryption on the Raspbery Pi.
